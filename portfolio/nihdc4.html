NIHDC4

Custom video player goes here

This was an motion design entry for the NIHD's annual contest 'NIHDC4'. The contests theme for this year was to go beyond what you already know and showcase everything you know on a specific program you use to make motion design."

In this project I had so much fun experimenting with effects, I learnt so much on how to combine them together effectively, as well as learning how to use null layers in other ways to create fake 3d in 2d such as at 0:00 to 0:04. Some 3d models designed in 2d in illustrator and were made 3d in After Effects.
